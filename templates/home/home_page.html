WHO IS "SCHOOL OF PSYCHOLOGY" FOR?

The course is suitable for amateurs, students or students (over 18 years old) who want to understand more about psychology AND the fundamental aspects of psychological science and gain knowledge about the most fundamental aspects of psychological science.

WHAT IS A "SCHOOL OF PSYCHOLOGY"?

The course includes 12 modules, selected from the various branches of psychology, so that students can get the broadest idea of the influence and application of psychology in our daily lives.
